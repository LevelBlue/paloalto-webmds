Directions to Use the Palo Alto Web MDS Form:
  • Start with Customer Details: Enter your name, email, and contact number under the Customer Information section.
  • Provide Affected User / Site Details: Fill in the impacted user or site information including name, email, contact, location, and source IP.
  • Complete the Troubleshooting Questionnaire: Answer all 15 questions related to offer type, connectivity type, SDWAN details if applicable, IPs, traceroutes, errors, and recent changes.
  • Describe the Issue Clearly: Include when it last worked, number of users affected, and troubleshooting already performed.
  • Copy to Clipboard: Once the form is filled out, click on the "Copy to Clipboard" button to copy all your responses into plain text and paste into the ticket.
  • Attach Evidence Separately: Traceroute outputs, screenshots, and logs should be attached directly in the support ticket.
  • Reset Option: Use the "Reset" button if you want to clear the form and start over.
